🔍 Quests

Quests is a Web application that is being developed for the professor of the System and Digital Media - UFC course, Ticanne Darin, to meet the demand for questionnaires from the Célula Multimídia research group for which she is responsible. The purpose of the application is to disseminate questionnaires and increase the adherence and engagement of respondents through a reward system. The platform's target audience are researchers, people responsible for collecting data through questionnaires, and respondents, people interested in answering questionnaires, thus having two user profiles, which can be combined: the researcher, who will use the platform to share the link to your questionnaire; and the respondent who, through the platform, will access the form's link, fill it out and accumulate rewards in his profile.
